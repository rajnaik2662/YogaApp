Importance of Yoga

Yoga is a physical, mental, and spiritual practice that originated in India. It helps in improving flexibility, strength, and overall wellness.

Types of Yoga

Hatha Yoga: Focuses on physical postures and breathing exercises.

Vinyasa Yoga: A dynamic style of yoga that connects movement and breath.

Ashtanga Yoga: A rigorous style with a fixed sequence of postures.

Yin Yoga: Focuses on long-held postures to increase flexibility.

Kundalini Yoga: Aims to awaken the dormant energy within.
